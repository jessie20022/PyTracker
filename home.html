A way for you to track your circadian rhythm

PyTracker is a webapp that will allow users to log and track their daily sleep hours and use a reaction time test to determine whether or not they should take a nap.

FEATURES

PyTracker will do many things for you. To begin, you'll be able to log into your account, which will track all of your data that you wish to enter.

By logging your sleep hours, PyTracker will be able to tell you show you your average hours slept, the median, least amount slept, most amount slept, and more! You can also choose to view your data annually or monthly.

In addition to logging your hours, you can take a reaction test. Once you take multiple tests at your prime energy, your data will be recorded. Any time you want to strengthen your scores, you can take the reaction test again. If you feel tired, you can take the reaction test and it will tell you whether or not you should sleep.

ABOUT US

We've created this webapp in order to help those who are curious about their sleep habits. PyTracker, written in Python, provides detailed statistics about one's sleep habits for free! Jessica, one of the founders, had logged her hours of sleep for over 1.5 years. Her idea to implement this webapp was not only to fulfil her curiousity of seeing her statistics, but also make it easier for her to see her sleep habits. William, our second founder, thought this was a great idea and joined in to help bring this webapp to others. Both founders are undergraduate students at the University of Maryland.
